Bros Boogies is a public playlist on Spotify that me and a couple friends curate. Every week, each friend submits 4 of their favorite songs and no two songs can have the same artist. I thought of Bros Boogies as a way to find new music ... I used to love Spotify's Discover Weekly playlist but it's a bit too personalized and songs are repeated in different weeks.
